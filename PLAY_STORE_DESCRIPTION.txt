FULL DESCRIPTION:

LilyNotes is a flexible note-taking app that lets you build personalized pages using a variety of widgets. Whether you want to track habits, manage tasks, monitor progress, or simply jot down notes, LilyNotes adapts to your needs.

WIDGETS FOR EVERY PURPOSE

Text Blocks
Write notes, ideas, and descriptions with rich text formatting.

Checklists
Create task lists and check off items as you complete them. Perfect for shopping lists, to-do lists, and daily tasks.

Habit Tracker
Build better habits by tracking your daily routines. See your progress at a glance and stay motivated.

Progress Bars
Set goals and monitor your progress visually. Great for projects, savings goals, or any milestone you want to reach.

Timers
Countdown timers and stopwatch functionality for workouts, cooking, study sessions, or any timed activity.

Score and Counter
Keep track of scores, counts, or any number you need to monitor.

Counter Lists
Multiple named counters in one widget. Track inventory, points, or anything that needs counting.

Expense Tracker
Log expenses and keep track of your spending by category.

Bookmarks
Save and organize your favorite links in one place.

Dividers
Organize your pages with visual separators.

WHY LILYNOTES?

- Privacy First: All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking.
- Offline: Works completely offline. Your notes are always accessible.
- Customizable: Build pages that work for you by combining different widgets.
- Simple: Clean, intuitive interface that gets out of your way.
- Free: No ads, no in-app purchases, no premium features locked behind a paywall.
