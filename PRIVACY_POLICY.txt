PRIVACY POLICY FOR LILYNOTES

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

LilyNotes ("we", "our", or "the app") is a widget-based note-taking application. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. DATA COLLECTION AND STORAGE

LilyNotes does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data you create within the app, including:
- Notes and text content
- Checklists and tasks
- Habit tracking data
- Progress tracking data
- Timer settings
- Bookmarks
- Expense records

is stored LOCALLY on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

3. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data for marketing purposes

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

LilyNotes does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. DATA SECURITY

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:
- Using device lock (PIN, password, or biometric)
- Keeping your device software updated
- Not sharing your device with untrusted parties

6. DATA DELETION

You can delete your data at any time by:
- Deleting individual items within the app
- Clearing the app's data through your device settings
- Uninstalling the app

When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

LilyNotes does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for users of all ages as it does not collect personal data.

8. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. CONTACT

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository:
https://github.com/TOMSLAUS/lilynotes

10. CONSENT

By using LilyNotes, you consent to this privacy policy.
